apart from gaining knowledge about web development.


Getting Started

To use this application, you need to sign in first. You can use any unique email id and password ...
If something goes wrong, that means you probably have provided some invalid email id or so.

After logging in, you will be redirected to your profile page where you will be able to see
your name , number of pending assignments , total number of assignments , etc.
Now go to 'Create' tab in the navigation bar , and fill in the details to create a new assignment. After
creating a new assignment, you will be redirected to the 'Show All' tab where you can see your newly created assignment.
If you intend to delete the assignment, you can press the delete option over there, or you can also edit it. That's pretty much
the whole thing. But, this is not the end. There are couple of things that I had planned on doing but I am lazy.
